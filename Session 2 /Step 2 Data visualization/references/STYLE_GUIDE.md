# WHU Template Studio Style Guide

This style guide is extracted from the bundled PowerPoint template in [assets/whu-overall-template.pptx](../assets/whu-overall-template.pptx).

## Canvas and grid
- Slide size: 13.333 × 7.5 in (16:9 widescreen).
- Keep the original placeholder boxes, margins, and alignments from the template. Replace text and images, do not redraw layouts.

## Typography
- Font family: **Arial** (major + minor theme fonts).
- Standard body copy: **16 pt**.
- Standard slide headline: **40 pt**, **bold**.
- Topic label (top line on content slides): **16 pt**, regular, light-blue.
- Sub-headline label (optional): **16 pt**, regular, light-blue.
- Slide number in footer: **13 pt**, **bold**.

### Title slides (cover variants)
- **WHU_4-Part Headline**:
  - Part 1: **62 pt**, **bold**, light-blue
  - Part 2: **48 pt**, **bold**, dark-blue
  - Author | Department: **16 pt**, dark-blue

- **WHU_Long Title + Image**:
  - Title lines: **40 pt**, **bold**, light-blue then dark-blue
  - Author | Department: **16 pt**, dark-blue

- **WHU_Long Title_Blue**:
  - Background: dark-blue
  - Title: **44 pt**, **bold**, white then light-blue
  - Author | Department: **16 pt**, white

## Color palette (hex)
- Dark blue: #054696
- Light blue: #00A5DC
- Light grey-blue: #E6EBF0
- Dark grey: #505459
- Green: #1CAD00
- Teal: #006E78
- Turquoise: #00AAB9
- Orange: #EB5A0A

## Background variants
- White: #FFFFFF
- Blue: #054696
- Light-blue: #00A5DC
- Silver-grey: #E6EBF0

## Charts and visuals
- Use Arial for all chart labels.
- Prefer 1 to 2 accent colors per chart:
  - Primary series: dark blue
  - Secondary series: light blue
  - Additional series: dark grey, light grey-blue
- Minimize visual noise: remove heavy gridlines, avoid 3D effects, keep labels concise.
